Our Camp

With hacking stories appearing daily in global news headlines, and a world that's more interconnected than ever, we need sharp minds focused on safeguarding online communities and capabilities. Students will love either the co-ed or all-girls, which are each hands-on and emphasis cyber ethics, online safety, cyber security and defense.
This RIT camp is funded by a federal grant from the NSA GenCyber program and is FREE for accepted students to attend.

Camp Info

2019-2020 Summer Camps

The will be TWO opportunities to participate in GenCyber at RIT this summer. We will offer a one-week coed camp and a one-week camp for girls. Both weeks will follow the same curriculum. GenCyber will be offered in collaboration with RIT's Camp Tiger on the RIT campus with RIT faculty, staff, and students serving as instructors. For more information on Camp Tiger please visit the Camp Tiger website.
